How to use from HA
After saving, go to HA → Settings → Devices & Services → Add Integration → ESPHome
Enter this PC's local IP address and port 6053.

HA exposes one switch per camera — turn a switch ON to show it, OFF to hide.
In an automation: switch.turn_on with your camera's switch entity.
